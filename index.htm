Corin Coetzee

I work in both functional hand-built forms - primarily pinched and coiled vessels and soft-slab building - and less traditional ones drawn from a variety of sources, including crochet, basket-weaving, and other textile techniques.

The top row consists of cup forms I have been experimenting with recently. The second row are more varied shapes including large vessels, sculpture, installation, video, and in-progress work.

My work tends to explore the passage of time, and the technology human societies produce to detect, account for, and control it.
